About Us

At Trade Ninja, we are truly passionate about early financial education. Founded in 2013, we specialize in investing and financial literacy education for teens and young adults. Too few high school curriculum include effective learning material that will get core financial concepts to stick with most teens. So we partnered with educators and game developers to create the first interactive online investment course for teens, Trade Ninja.

Feel free to contact us via email at onehourinvestor@gmail.com

Our Products

Trade Ninja is online financial education tool for teens and young adults. It incorporates interactive learning sessions, action/reward activities, and a portfolio simulation tool.

Our Educational version also allows class teachers to keep track of student progress and implement learning activities through the application. Trade Ninja is designed to keep young adults engaged and interested and is guaranteed to give everyone the tools they need to be intelligent investors in the future.
